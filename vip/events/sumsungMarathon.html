Samsung Marathon

Tel aviv February 2018

Info

the Tel Aviv Samsung Marathon is a leading sport event to take place again this spring in one of the most exciting Mediterranean cities.

The Tel Samsung Marathon is a multi-course event allowing runners in all levels and styles to take part. It is yearly citywide celebration designed for professional long-distance runners as well as beginners.

This year's marathon offers the following courses: Marathon, Half Marathon, 10k, 5k, and a Kids Mini Marathon.
